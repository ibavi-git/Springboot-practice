SIVAJI: The Boss

Sivaji: The Boss is a 2007 Indian Tamil-language action film directed by S. Shankar and starring Rajinikanth in the lead role. The film follows the story of Sivaji, a software engineer who returns to India after studying abroad and decides to use his wealth to help the poor and fight against corruption.

The film was a commercial success and received positive reviews for its action sequences, music, and performances. It also won several awards, including the Filmfare Award for Best Actor for Rajinikanth.
